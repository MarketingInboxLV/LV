Popular common questions and your answer

001
Do you design for both mobile and web applications?

Yes, we design for all types of applications including native mobile apps (iOS and Android), web applications, progressive web apps (PWAs), and cross platform solutions. We ensure consistent user experience across all platforms while optimizing each design for the specific strengths and constraints of each platform. Our designs follow platform specific guidelines (Material Design for Android, Human Interface Guidelines for iOS) while maintaining your brand identity.

002
What's included in your application design process?

Our comprehensive application design includes user research and personas, information architecture, user flow mapping, wireframing, visual interface design, interactive prototyping, and design system creation. We also provide detailed design specifications for developers, icon sets, and all necessary assets in various formats. Additionally, we offer usability testing and design validation to ensure optimal user experience.

003
How do you ensure the app design will be user-friendly and intuitive?

We follow a user centered design approach that begins with thorough user research to understand your target audience's needs, behaviors, and pain points. We create user personas, map user journeys, and develop wireframes that prioritize ease of use. We also build interactive prototypes for testing and validation, allowing us to identify and resolve usability issues before final implementation.

004
Can you work with our development team or do you provide development services too?

We can work seamlessly with your existing development team, providing detailed design specifications, style guides, and ongoing design support throughout the development process. While we specialize in design, we also offer web development services through our development team. We're flexible and can adapt to your preferred workflow, whether you need design only services or full design to development solutions.

005
How long does the application design process typically take?

The timeline depends on application complexity and scope. A simple mobile app design typically takes 6-8 weeks, while complex applications with multiple user types and advanced features can take 10-16 weeks. This includes research, wireframing, visual design, prototyping, and revisions. We provide detailed project timelines during the planning phase and maintain regular communication throughout the design process to ensure timely delivery.
