Creative Landscaping & Outdoor Design

Clients

Juniper Hayes

Category

Creative Landscaping & Outdoor Design

Date

06 April 2025

Location

Idaho, USA

Project Overview:

This landscaping company specialized in transforming ordinary outdoor spaces into stunning entertainment areas and peaceful retreats, but their portfolio was hidden behind poor photography and disorganized presentation. We created a visually stunning website that captures the seasonal beauty of their work through professional photography and virtual garden tours that help clients envision their space's potential.

Our marketing strategy focused on Pinterest and Instagram integration, local home and garden show partnerships, and seasonal content campaigns. We developed educational resources about plant care, outdoor living trends, and sustainable landscaping practices that established them as the go to experts for luxury outdoor transformations, resulting in 300% growth in consultation requests and 85% higher project values.

Challenge & Solution

Challenge

The landscaping company's work was seasonal and weather dependent, creating unpredictable income streams and difficulty maintaining year round client engagement. Their previous website failed to convey the long-term value of professional landscaping, and most visitors couldn't visualize how their services would transform ordinary yards into outdoor living spaces that increase property value.

Storytelling & Design

We created a narrative around outdoor lifestyle enhancement and property investment, using time lapse videos of garden transformations and seasonal progression galleries. The design mirrors natural elements with organic shapes, earth tones, and flowing layouts that feel like walking through a garden. Interactive features include a plant selector tool, maintenance calendar, and augmented reality preview that lets visitors visualize landscaping options in their own yards.
