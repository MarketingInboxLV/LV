Popular common questions and your answer

001
What technologies and platforms do you use for web development?

We work with a wide range of modern technologies including HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, WordPress, Shopify, and custom PHP development. We also specialize in popular platforms like Webflow for design focused websites and can recommend the best technology stack based on your specific needs, budget, and long term goals. Our team stays updated with the latest web standards and frameworks to ensure your website is built with cutting edge technology.

002
Will my website be mobile-friendly and work on all devices?

Absolutely! All our websites are built with a responsive design approach, meaning they automatically adapt and look great on smartphones, tablets, desktops, and all screen sizes. We test extensively across different devices and browsers to ensure consistent functionality and user experience. Mobile optimization is standard in all our projects, not an additional feature.

003
How long does it take to build a website?

Timeline varies based on project complexity and requirements. A standard business website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while e-commerce sites or custom web applications can take 8-12 weeks. We provide detailed project timelines during the planning phase and keep you updated on progress throughout development. Factors affecting timeline include number of pages, custom features, content preparation, and revision rounds.

004
Do you provide website maintenance and support after launch?

Yes, we offer comprehensive post launch support including regular updates, security monitoring, backup services, and technical assistance. We provide different maintenance packages to suit various needs and budgets. Our ongoing support ensures your website remains secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. We also offer content updates and feature additions as your business evolves.

005
Can you help with website hosting and domain setup?

Definitely! We can assist with domain registration, hosting setup, and technical configuration to get your website live. We work with reliable hosting providers and can recommend the best hosting solution based on your website's requirements and expected traffic. We also handle SSL certificates, email setup, and all technical aspects to ensure your website launches smoothly and securely.
